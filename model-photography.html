Model Photography

By CineTorqueFilms • Cinematic Workflow

Model photography is not about the model — it's about control over light, motion, and emotion.

1. Lighting First

Before you even touch the camera, understand your light. Natural light creates softness, while hard light builds contrast and drama.

2. Movement Matters

A static subject is boring. Introduce slight motion — hair movement, fabric flow, or even micro-expressions.

3. Lens Choice

Your lens defines your story. Wide lenses exaggerate space. Telephoto compresses emotion.

4. My Workflow

Shoot → Select → Color Grade → Refine. Keep it simple but intentional.

At the end, it’s not about gear — it’s about vision.
