Model Photography

By CineTorqueFilms • 6 min read

Model photography is more than just capturing a person — it's about shaping light, expression, and emotion into a frame that feels alive.

Lighting the Subject

Lighting defines the mood of your image. A soft key light creates smooth skin tones, while harder light can add contrast and drama.

ISO: 100

Aperture: f/2.8

Shutter: 1/125

Lens & Perspective

Your lens choice changes how the subject feels. A 50mm lens gives a natural look, while 85mm compresses the background for a cinematic feel.

“A great portrait is not taken — it is revealed.”

Working with the Model

Communication is key. Guide your model with small adjustments — eye direction, posture, and movement create natural, powerful frames.
