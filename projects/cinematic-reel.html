Director's Commentary

Why this shot: A cinematic reel needs to immediately prove technical capability and artistic vision. This opening sequence sets the tone for the entire portfolio.

Camera Setup: Various cinema cameras including RED, Sony FX3, and Arri Alexa Mini LF across multiple setups.

Lighting: A mix of heavy studio lighting, natural run-and-gun outdoor lighting, and neon practical effects.

Mistakes & Fixes: Matching color grades from so many different cameras was difficult. We used a standardized film emulation LUT to tie everything into one cohesive look.
