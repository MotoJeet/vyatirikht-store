Director's Commentary

Why this shot: The environment of an MX Race is chaotic and fast. We wanted to drop the viewer directly into the dust and speed.

Camera Setup: Sony A7SIII with a 70-200mm f/2.8 lens to compress the background and stay safe from flying rocks.

Lighting: Harsh mid-day sun. Rather than fighting it, we leaned into the high contrast to exaggerate the gritty feel.

Mistakes & Fixes: Dirt constantly coated the UV filter, ruining shots mid-race. We switched to an air-blower between laps to keep it clear.
