Director's Commentary

Why this shot: The goal was to portray the Xenon as a rugged, unstoppable force in its natural environment.

Camera Setup: RED Komodo mounted on a Blackarm to absorb the violent shakes of the tracking vehicle.

Lighting: Overcast daylight acting as a giant softbox, allowing us to keep details in the shadows of the truck's grill.

Mistakes & Fixes: Keeping the tracking vehicle out of the reflections of the truck was tough. We used a CPL filter to completely kill the paint reflections.
