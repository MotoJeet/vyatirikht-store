Director's Commentary

Why this shot: The goal was to sculpt the physique of the athletes using the existing edgy atmosphere of the gym, highlighting the architecture and mood.

Camera Setup: Sony FX3 with a 24-70mm G-Master Lens to quickly adapt to different framing needs in a busy environment.

Lighting: Existing practical lights augmented with a small RGB tube light held off-camera for rim-lighting the sweat.

Mistakes & Fixes: We encountered heavy flickering from the cheap overhead fluorescent lights. We dialed our shutter angle to 144 degrees to sync and eliminate the flicker.
