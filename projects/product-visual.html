Director's Commentary

Why this shot: We needed to showcase the sleek, metallic body of the Sony A6700 while maintaining a moody, premium commercial aesthetic.

Camera Setup: RED Komodo 6K with Laowa 24mm Probe Lens.

Lighting: Studio setup using Aputure 300d with a softbox and negative fill to carve out the edges of the camera.

Mistakes & Fixes: Reflections on the camera lens were too harsh initially. We flagged off the light source and used a polarizer to control the glare.
